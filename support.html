Frequently Asked Questions

What is stooping?
Stooping means giving usable items a second life instead of sending them to the landfill. Everything on Stooping Club is free and picked up locally in Berkeley.

Are the items really free?
Yes — everything listed in the app is free. There are no prices, fees, or purchases.

Where do items come from?
Items come from curbside finds and community donations, from people and organizations decluttering, moving, or clearing out excess inventory.

Do you ship orders?
No, pickup only. All items must be picked up in person in the Berkeley area.

How does pickup work?
Once you claim an item in the app, you'll get pickup details by text or email. Because space is limited, unclaimed pickups are relisted after a few days if not picked up.

Is it first come, first served?
Yes. Add an item to your cart and complete checkout to claim it before someone else does.

Is there a limit on how many items I can order?
There's no hard limit, but we ask everyone to be mindful so more people can benefit.

Can I resell items I get from Stooping Club?
No — items are for personal use or gifting only, not resale.

Do you accept returns?
Since everything is free, all claims are final and we don't accept returns. Please review photos and descriptions carefully before claiming. If something's wrong with an item, reach out and we'll help however we can.

What if an item is no longer available?
Items are removed once they're claimed, so availability isn't guaranteed until checkout is complete.

What condition are the items in?
All items are pre-owned, used, or open-box, and we do our best to make sure everything listed is functional and in good, usable condition.

How often is new inventory added?
New items are added weekly, so check the app often for the latest drops.

Can I donate items?
Yes — we accept gently used or new household items that are still usable. We reserve the right to decline items we're unable to accept.

Do you accept large items like furniture?
We can't store large items, but in some cases we can still list them. If someone claims your item, we'll help connect you so it can be picked up directly from you.

How do I delete my account?
Email us at the address above and we'll remove your account and associated data.
