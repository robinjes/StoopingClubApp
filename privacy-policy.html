Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Stooping Club ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Stooping Club mobile app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your information.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the app:

  • Account information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password (processed securely through our commerce platform, Shopify). This is used to identify you and manage your account.
  • Order and activity information: Information about items you claim, order history, and wishlist activity within the app.
  • Push notification tokens: If you enable push notifications, we store a device token so we can send you updates (e.g., new item drops, order status).

We do not collect your location, and we do not use any analytics or crash-reporting tools.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process and track orders/claims
  • To send you push notifications, email, or SMS updates about your orders or new inventory (only if you opt in)

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with:

  • Shopify, which powers our account authentication and order processing
  • Service providers who help us send order-related emails or SMS messages, where applicable

Data Retention

We retain your account and order information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services to you.

Your Choices

  • You can disable push notifications at any time in your device settings.
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

Stooping Club is not intended for use by children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
